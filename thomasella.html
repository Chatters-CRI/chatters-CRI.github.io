Ella started her PhD at Human Media Interaction in October 2019. She holds a MSc in Information Systems from the University of Amsterdam in 2019. Prior to that, she obtained a BA in Communication and Information Sciences from the VU Amsterdam. During these studies, she got interested in the intersection of human communication and technology research. Her PhD focuses on responsible child-robot interaction, with a special interest in voice (of the robot as well as the child) and trust.

When she's not working, she can often be found in the kitchen or on the couch. She loves to cook fancy dinners and bake cakes. She also likes reading, going to the movies, watching my cats, snorkling, and walking alongside the IJssel, a river near my house.
